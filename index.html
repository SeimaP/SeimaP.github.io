‚ù§Ô∏è Health Conditions (Izifo)

1. HIV

Symptoms: HIV may have no signs at first. Later: weight loss, night sweats, tiredness, mouth sores, infections. Get tested.
Izimpawu: I-HIV ayinalo uphawu ekuqaleni. Kamuva kuba ukukhathala, ukuncipha umzimba, umkhuhlane wamabele, izilonda. Eya ekliniki.
Prevention: Use condoms, get tested often, don‚Äôt share needles or blades. Take PrEP if at risk. Stick to one partner.
Ukuvimbela: Sebenzisa ikondomu. Hlola njalo. Ungabelani ngemibese noma izinaliti. Thatha iPrEP uma usesengozini. Beka umlingani oyedwa.
Treatment: No cure, but ARVs help you live a long life. Take daily. Get treatment free at the clinic. Don‚Äôt skip doses.
Ukwelashwa: Ayipholiswa kodwa ama-ARV ayasiza ukuphila isikhathi eside. Phuza nsuku zonke. Hamba ekliniki. Ungaphutheli umthamo.

2. TB (Tuberculosis) / Isifo sofuba

Symptoms: Cough for 2+ weeks, chest pain, fever, weight loss, night sweats, coughing blood. Go to clinic if symptoms stay.
Izimpawu: Ukukhwehlela amasonto angu-2 noma ngaphezulu, ubuhlungu besifuba, umkhuhlane, izinzwa ebusuku, ukwehla kwesisindo. Iya ekliniki.
Prevention: Cover mouth when coughing, open windows, avoid close contact. Finish your TB treatment fully if you start.
Ukuvimbela: Vala umlomo uma ukhwihilizela. Vula amafasitela. Gwema ukusondela kwabantu. Qeda umjikelezo wemithi ka-TB ngokuphelele.
Treatment: Free meds at clinic for 6 months. Take every day. Don‚Äôt stop even if you feel better ‚Äî that makes TB stronger.
Ukwelashwa: Uthola imithi mahhala ekliniki izinyanga eziyisi-6. Phuza nsuku zonke. Ungayeki noma uzizwa ungcono, i-TB ingabuya iqine.

3. Diabetes (Isifo sikashukela)

Symptoms: Always thirsty, pee often, tired, blurry vision, slow-healing cuts, lose weight fast. These may be signs of diabetes.
Izimpawu: Uhlale womile, uya endlini njalo, ukukhathala, ukubona kabi, amanxeba angelapheki, ukwehla kwesisindo. Ukuya ekliniki.
Prevention: Eat healthy, less sugar, more veggies. Exercise often. Drink water. Test often, especially if family has diabetes.
Ukuvimbela: Yidla kahle. Nciphisa ushukela. Yenza ezemidlalo. Phuza amanzi. Hlola igazi uma umndeni unalesi sifo.
Treatment: Get pills or insulin at the clinic. Eat well, avoid sugar, move your body, and check blood sugar when you can.
Ukwelashwa: Uthola amaphilisi noma i-insulin ekliniki. Yidla kahle, gwema ushukela, qeqesha umzimba, hlola ushukela njalo.

4. Hypertension (High Blood Pressure) / Ingcindezi ephezulu

Symptoms: Often silent. May cause dizziness, headaches, blurry vision, chest pain. Check BP often, don‚Äôt wait for signs.
Izimpawu: Ayinazo izimpawu eziningi. Kungaba yikhanda, ukubona kabi, ukuphelelwa amandla. Hlola i-BP njalo, ungasilindi isibonakaliso.
Prevention: Eat less salt, no fatty foods, stop smoking, stay active, manage stress. Walk daily and drink water.
Ukuvimbela: Gwema usawoti, ukudla okunamafutha. Tshiya ugwayi. Zilolonge. Nciphisa ukucindezeleka. Phuza amanzi.
Treatment: Take BP pills daily from clinic. Don‚Äôt skip doses. Eat healthy, exercise, check BP often. Treatment is lifelong.
Ukwelashwa: Phuza amaphilisi we-BP nsuku zonke ekliniki. Ungaphutheli. Yidla kahle, zilolonge, hlola i-BP njalo. Ukwelashwa kuphila isikhathi sonke.

üòå Emotional Well-being (Imizwa)

1. Anxious: Breathe. You have survived every hard day so far. This moment will pass. You are safe and stronger than you feel.
1. Ukukhathazeka: Phefumula. Usinde kuzo zonke izinsuku ezinzima. Lena izodlula. Uphila. Uphakamile kunalokho ozwa ngakho.
2. Depressed: Your pain is real but so is hope. Take small steps. You matter more than you know. You are not alone.
2. Ukudangala: Ubuhlungu bakho buyiqiniso, ithemba likhona. Thatha izinyathelo ezincane. Awuwedwa. Ubalulekile kakhulu.
3. Angry: Take a deep breath. Use that energy to build, not break. You have control. You are not your anger.
3. Ukuthukuthela: Phefumula. Usebenzise amandla akho ukwakha hhayi ukubhidliza. Ulawula wena. Awuyona intukuthelo yakho.
4. Lonely: You are not forgotten. Even if you feel alone, you are loved. This world is better with you in it.
4. Ukweswela abangane: Awukho wedwa. Ngisho noma uzizwa kanjalo, unothando. Umhlaba uhlakaniphile ngokuba nawe kuwo.
5. Tired: Rest is not weakness. You have been strong for too long. Take time to breathe. You are allowed to pause.
5. Ukukhathala: Ukuphumula akusona ubuthakathaka. Ubelokhu uqine isikhathi eside. Phefumula. Ukuphumula kuyavunyelwa.
6. Hopeless: Dark nights do end. Do not give up before the breakthrough. Light is coming. Keep breathing. Keep holding on.
6. Ukuphelelwa ithemba: Ubumnyama buyadlula. Ungayeki ngaphambi kokuphumelela. Ukukhanya kuyaqhamuka. Qhubeka uphefumula.
7. Confused: It is okay to not have the answers. Clarity comes with time. One small step is still progress.
7. Ukudideka: Kulungile ukungabi nezimpendulo. Ukucaca kuza nesikhathi. Inyathelo elincane nalo libalulekile.
8. Stressed: You cannot control everything, but you can take one deep breath. Then take another. You are doing your best.
8. Ukucindezeleka: Awukwazi ukulawula konke. Kodwa ungaphefumula kanye bese futhi. Wenza konke okusemandleni akho.
9. Empty: Feeling numb does not mean you are broken. Sometimes healing is just surviving the day. That is enough.
9. Ukungazizwa: Ukungazizwa akusho ukuthi uphukile. Ukuphila kosuku olulodwa nakho kuyindlela yokululama.
10. Grieving: Your loss matters. So does your healing. Cry if you need to. Love lives on through memory.
10. Ukulila: Ilahleko yakho ibalulekile. Kanye nokululama kwakho. Khala uma kudingeka. Uthando luhlala ngezikumbuzo.

‚ôÄÔ∏è Period Information (Imininingwane Ngezinyanga)

1. What is a period? A period is when blood leaves the uterus through the vagina each month. It shows the body is ready to have babies.
1. Kuyini ukuya enyangeni? Ukuya enyangeni kwenzeka nxa igazi liphuma esibelethweni njalo ngenyanga. Kusho ukuthi umzimba usukulungele ukuthwala ingane.
2. How long it lasts: A period lasts 3 to 7 days. It comes once a month but can change for each person. It is normal to miss one sometimes.
2. Kuhlala isikhathi esingakanani? Ihlala izinsuku ezi-3 kuya kweziyi-7. Iza njalo ngenyanga kodwa ingashintsha kwabanye. Kulungile ukuyiphuthelwa kwesinye isikhathi.
3. Symptoms: You may feel tired, get cramps, mood swings, sore breasts, or back pain. Some people also get pimples or feel bloated.
3. Izimpawu: Ungakhathala, uzwe izinhlungu emhlane noma esiswini, ube nezinhlungu ezibuhlungu noma uzizwe ugcwele noma uphuphume izinduna.
4. Pain relief tips: Use a hot water bottle, drink water, rest, or take pain pills from the clinic. Exercise and eating healthy can help too.
4. Izindlela zokuthoba izinhlungu: Sebenzisa ibhodlela elishisayo, phuza amanzi, phumula, thatha amaphilisi ekliniki. Ukuzivocavoca nokudla kahle kuyasiza.
5. Can I get pregnant? Yes. You can get pregnant even on your period. Sperm can live inside the body for days. Use protection every time.
5. Ingabe ngingakhulelwa? Yebo. Ungakhulelwa nasenkathini yakho. Isidoda sihlala emzimbeni izinsuku. Sebenzisa ivikelo njalo.

ü§î Myth or Truth Game (Umdlalo Weqiniso Nenganekwane)

1. Myth: "If both partners have HIV, they can‚Äôt reinfect each other."
Answer: False. Even if both partners have HIV, unsafe sex can lead to HIV reinfection with a stronger strain. Always use protection.
1. Inganekwane: "Uma bobabili bene HIV abakwazi ukusulelelanisa futhi."
Impendulo: Amanga. Noma nobabili nine HIV, ucansi olungaphephile lungabangela uhlobo olusha oluqinile. Sebenzisa ikondomu njalo.
2. Statement: "You can have a baby without passing HIV to the child."
Answer: True. Yes. If the mother takes ARVs and follows clinic care, the baby can be born HIV-negative. Always deliver at a clinic.
2. Isitatimende: "Ungazala ingane ngaphandle kokuyithelela iHIV."
Impendulo: Iqiniso. Yebo. Uma umama ethatha ama-ARV futhi aye ekliniki, ingane ingazalwa ingenayo i-HIV. Zala esibhedlela kuphela.
3. Myth: "If you feel better, you can stop TB meds."
Answer: False. Never stop TB meds early. Even if you feel better, you must finish the 6 months or TB can come back stronger.
3. Inganekwane: "Uma usuzizwa ungcono, ungayeka imithi ye TB."
Impendulo: Amanga. Ungayeki imithi ye TB noma usuzizwa ungcono. Qeda izinyanga eziyisithupha noma isifo sizobuya siqine.
4. Myth: "Only fat people get diabetes."
Answer: False. Anyone can get diabetes, even thin people. Family history, poor diet, and little exercise are big risks.
4. Inganekwane: "Abantu abakhuluphele kuphela abathola iDiabetes."
Impendulo: Amanga. Noma ubani angayithola i-Diabetes. Ukudla kabi, ukungazivocavoci, nomlando womndeni kuyingozi enkulu.
5. Myth: "You can feel when your blood pressure is high."
Answer: False. Most people feel nothing. That is why it is called the "silent killer." Always check your BP at the clinic.
5. Inganekwane: "Uzokwazi uma ingcindezi igcwele emzimbeni wakho."
Impendulo: Amanga. Abaningi abakwazi lutho. I-BP ibizwa ngesibulala buthule. Hlola i-BP yakho njalo ekliniki.
6. Myth: "You cannot get pregnant on your period."
Answer: False. You can get pregnant during your period, especially if your cycle is short. Use protection every time.
6. Inganekwane: "Awukwazi ukukhulelwa uma une period."
Impendulo: Amanga. Ungakhulelwa nasenkathini yakho, ikakhulukazi uma umjikelezo mfushane. Sebenzisa ivikelo njalo.
7. Myth: "Depression is just sadness and you must snap out of it."
Answer: False. Depression is an illness, not just sadness. You cannot just "snap out of it." Get help and support from a clinic or SADAG.
7. Inganekwane: "IDepression ukudabuka kuphela ungakuyeka."
Impendulo: Amanga. I-Depression isifo, hhayi ukudabuka nje. Udinga usizo nolwelapheko. Hamba ekliniki noma shayela i-SADAG.
8. Myth: "If you have anxiety, you are weak."
Answer: False. Anxiety is not weakness. It is a health condition and can be managed with support, breathing, and sometimes treatment.
8. Inganekwane: "Ukukhathazeka kubonisa ubuthakathaka."
Impendulo: Amanga. Ukukhathazeka akusona isici. Kungelashwa. Ngamanye amahora kudinga usizo.
9. Statement: "You can date someone with HIV and never get it."
Answer: True. Yes. With condoms and PrEP, HIV cannot be passed. HIV is not a death sentence. Love safely and smartly.
9. Isitatimende: "Ungathandana nomuntu one HIV ungayitholi."
Impendulo: Iqiniso. Yebo. Uma usebenzisa ikondomu noma i-PrEP, ngeke usuleleke. I-HIV ayisona isigwebo sokufa. Thandana ngokuhlakanipha.
10. Myth: "Drinking alcohol helps you relax, so it‚Äôs good for stress."
Answer: False. Alcohol may feel relaxing, but it makes stress worse over time and harms your health. Get real help if you feel overwhelmed.
10. Inganekwane: "Utshwala busiza ukunciphisa ingcindezi."
Impendulo: Amanga. Utshwala abunciphisi ingcindezi. Njengoba isikhathi sihamba, lwenza isimo sibe sibi. Thola usizo lwangempela.
